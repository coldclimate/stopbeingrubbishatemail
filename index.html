Stop being rubbish at email

You've probably been sent a link here by a friend. They're trying to help you.

Learn to use BCC

When you CC or Carbon Copy everyone into an email they can all see each others email addresses. If they all know each other, or this is a group discusion this is fine, but where they don't know one another and might not appreciate having their personal details shared, this is bad news. You've just crapped all over their privacy.

Blind Carbon Copy means that everybody included can see who emailed them (you) but not each other. Use it

Don't attach huge files

You might be using broadband but your email might be read on a mobile, or a poor connection and trying to download the huge Word document full of photos you've just attached might take a week. People also have limited amounts of email they can store in their inbox, and one chunky attachment might just blow that

DON'T WRITE IN ALL CAPS

It's not more readable, it's not clearer, it doesn't even highlight the important bits. IT'S LIKE BEING SHOUTED AT.

Your signiture is never going to be read

Massive signatures with address, email, website, motto, advice, tips, explanatory notes, images and a helpful reminder not to print this email (Save the trees!) are rarely read. Your company might require you to have a legal disclaimer but the more you add the less it will be read.

Don't forward circulars without chekcing them

It is unlikely there is a terrible new virus which is going to spread slower than you can forward this email. Disney are not giving a $1 to everybody who forward an email. There is not a a perfect salesman in your area using rape drugs. Check your sources before forwarding such emails, Snopes is an excellent reference.
